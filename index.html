About SergerPro

Founded in 2020 by master seamstress Maria Rodriguez, SergerPro emerged from a passion for helping sewists overcome their fear of serger machines. What started as local workshops has grown into a comprehensive online platform serving thousands of students worldwide.

Our mission is to empower creative individuals with the skills and confidence to create professional-quality garments and accessories. We believe that with the right guidance, anyone can master their serger and unlock endless possibilities in their sewing journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course suitable for complete beginners?

Absolutely! We start with the basics of machine setup and threading, gradually progressing to more advanced techniques. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, ensuring you're never overwhelmed.

What type of serger do I need for this course?

The techniques taught work with any 3 or 4-thread serger. We include specific tips for popular brands like Brother, Singer, and Juki, but the principles apply to all models.

How long does it take to complete the course?

The core curriculum takes about 12 hours to complete, but you can progress at your own pace. Many students complete it within 2-3 weeks while practicing the techniques.

Do you offer support if I get stuck?

Yes! You'll have access to our private community forum where you can ask questions, share projects, and get help from instructors and fellow students.

What's included in the lifetime access?

You get unlimited access to all current course materials, future updates, bonus projects, troubleshooting guides, and our private community forum.
